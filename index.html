ABOUT US

We are focused on providing a simple platform for farmers and customers to trade easily.

Our community has a core interest to serve as a catalyst to institutions and individuals,

in agriculture and rural development to share knowledge.

We aim at improving agriculture as a whole targeting all kind of farmers.

Consequently,we offer extension services to farmers where we teach about various farming methods,

fertilizer application,poultry and livestock farming.With our highly competent veterinary officers we work with,

we offer veterinary services including:

  1. Clinical Services including treatment of diseased animals and control of production thus limiting disorders.
  2. Preventive Services,that is avoiding and controlling outbreak of diseases.
  3. Provision of drugs and vaccines.
  4. Artificial Insemination.
  5. Human health protection.

SERVICES

CROP
FARMING

We give information on crop cultivation methods and climatic conditions.

We offer advice to farmers concerning starting poultry and livestock projects.

We also provide a very ready market for farmers.We help farmers reach their maximum profits

Services offered include vaccination and deworming.We ensure production of high breed livestock that are highly productive with high milk yields foe dairy cattle,

by providing Artificial Insemination(A.I).We promote exotic breeds eg Frieshians and Jersey.
